The file contains the customer list. Here are the column descriptions

Index :  mere unique id.
Customer Id :  unique ID for customers.
First Name :  first name of the customer
Last Name :  last name of the customer
Company : name of the company the customer belongs to
City : city customer is from
Country : country customer is from
Phone 1 : Customer's primary or first phone number
Phone 2 : customer's secondary phone number
Email : Email id of the customer
Subscription Date : The date when customer subscribed to our service. smaller the date, longer customer has stayed with us - implying loyalty
Website : website of customer
